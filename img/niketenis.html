Nike Paris Collection

Embracing the city's renowned fashion culture, the Nike Paris Collection pays homage to the iconic world of haute couture. Witness these breathtaking Nike designs firsthand on the Paris clay, and seize the opportunity to unleash your inner showstopper by trying them out yourself!

Read Into the Lines

In addition to their trendy style, the stripes showcased on the Nike Paris pieces are "topological lines", marking the direction of skin tension. They represent the natural orientation of collagen fibers in the skin, and are parallel to the position of the underlying muscles. The result is top-notch performance for all tennis players, both on match days and training sessions.
